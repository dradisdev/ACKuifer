  uses PFHpA (Perfluoroheptanoic acid, CAS 375-85-9), not PFHpS. Confirmed 
  against official MassDEP compound list. Laserfiche scraper is CORRECT using 
  PFHpA. Source Discovery prototype parsers (source_discovery_parser.py and 
  pace_lab_parser.py) have a bug using PFHpS — corrected in massdep.py to use 
  PFHpA. Full PFAS6 list: PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, PFNA, PFHpA, PFDA.
