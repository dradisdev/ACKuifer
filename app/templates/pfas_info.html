Massachusetts PFAS6 Standard: The Massachusetts Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL) for the sum of six PFAS compounds (PFAS6) in drinking water is 20 parts per trillion (ppt). Results at or below 20 ppt meet the state standard. Results above 20 ppt exceed it.
